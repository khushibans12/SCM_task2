Group Motivation

– While fitness trackers are centered around you as an individual with the objective of helping you meet your goals and personal milestones, some people enjoy the social features that some fitness trackers can provide. Sleep Tracking

– Trackers are now used to determine how well you are sleeping by monitoring your movement while asleep. Getting three full cycles of sleep in per night is important to maintaining your energy levels and giving your brain, and the rest of your body, the time it needs to recover and process everything that happened in the day prior.
